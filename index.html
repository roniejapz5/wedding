How they meet

They first met at the Parian Fire Station. Ann was on detached service at the Cebu City Fire Station starting February 16, 2021, a period marked by a shortage of personnel due to many firefighters undergoing their Fire Basic Recruit Course (FBRC), which stretched over five months. To fill the gaps in operations, temporary help was needed every Saturday and Sunday. It was during this time that Jill, a recent FBRC graduate and former member of the Parian team, joined them.

Little did Ann and Jill realize that what began as a temporary assignment would ignite something permanent and beautiful.
