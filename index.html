How we meet

We met at the Parian Fire Station. I was on detached service at the Cebu City Fire Station starting February 16, 2021, due to the need for additional personnel while many were undergoing their Fire Basic Recruit Course (FBRC), which lasted more than five months. During this period, the Cebu City Fire Station required temporary personnel to assist with operations every Saturday and Sunday. Jill, a fresh graduate from the FBRC training in April and a former member of the Parian team, joined us. Little did we know that this temporary assignment would spark something permanent and beautiful.
